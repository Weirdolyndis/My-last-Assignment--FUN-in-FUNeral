About Us

In My Last Assignment we believe in honoring every individual’s life through respect, understanding and kindness. We respect your choice and way of honoring death. We understand the importance of listening and preparing according to that choice, as well as doing our best to understand your condition, including your wishes and financial status. We highly value kindness through every service we provide, and vow to have you be comforted by it. Death is the last honorable assignment for us all. By honoring the end, we honor the beginning, and appreciate everything in between.
